# Revenue Model

> This is a living document. We haven't locked in a model yet — use this to capture ideas and decisions as they emerge.

---

## Ideas On The Table

### 1. Featured Listings / Promotion
- Businesses pay to be featured (homepage, top of category, etc.)
- Keep base listings free — paid = amplification, not access
- Keeps "No Pay-to-Play" ethos if base access stays free

### 2. Lead Generation / Referral Fees
- Route booking/inquiry leads to businesses with affiliate tracking
- High-value for charters, rentals, guides (Fish, Services, Beach)
- 🔑 affiliates (JOY Cart Rentals, Winton's, Port A Maintenance) = Tier 1 routing

### 3. Booking / Reservation Engine
- For Fish (charters), Beach (cabana setups), Services (golf carts)
- Take a % of each booking
- Our cabana service = OUR product (100% margin)
- JOY Cart Rentals = priority booking partner

### 4. Rental Engine
- Golf carts, beach equipment, water sports gear
- Our mobile beach cabana service is the anchor product here

### 5. Business Portal / Maintenance SaaS
- Businesses pay monthly for listing management, updates, lead dashboard
- Port A Maintenance Services (John Brown) = anchor customer for services portal

### 6. Advertising
- Banner/sponsored placement for seasonal events, businesses
- Lower priority but passive revenue

---

## Priority Order (Current Thinking)
1. Get directory live and populated → establish traffic/credibility
2. Activate lead gen for 🔑 affiliates → first revenue
3. Build booking engine (Fish + Beach) → scalable revenue
4. Add featured listings for willing businesses → promotion revenue
5. Portal/SaaS for business management → recurring revenue
