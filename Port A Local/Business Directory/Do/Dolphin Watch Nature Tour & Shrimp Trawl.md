## Description

The Dolphin Watch Nature Tour & Shrimp Trawl is one of Port Aransas's most beloved family experiences. Board a working shrimp trawl boat, haul in a net full of Gulf sea life, and spot wild dolphins along the way. Educational, hands-on, and genuinely fun for all ages.
