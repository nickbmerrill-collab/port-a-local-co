## About
One of the oldest continuously operating hotels on the Texas coast. Famous for its collection of over 7,000 tarpon scales signed by anglers dating back to the 1900s. Teddy Roosevelt was a guest. Home to [[Roosevelt's at The Tarpon Inn]].
