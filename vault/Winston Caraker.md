# Winston Caraker

Co-founder of [[The Palm Republic]] and Port A Local Co. Lifelong Port Aransas resident.

## Roles
- Co-founded [[The Palm Republic]] with Collie Farley after Hurricane Harvey (2017)
- Co-creator of Port A Local Co business directory
- Test user for iOS task dispatch app

## The Palm Republic Story
Winston took the sunset photograph that became the foundation of The Palm Republic's entire brand identity — the colors were pulled directly from that image. He designed the upright palm tree flag symbolizing resilience after the hurricane.
