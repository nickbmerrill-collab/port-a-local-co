# The Palm Republic

> *The Official Brand of Port Aransas*

The Palm Republic is a coastal lifestyle apparel brand founded by **Collie Farley** and **[[Winston Caraker]]** after Hurricane Harvey (2017).

## Brand Origin
Winston took a **sunset photograph** the first night they returned to Port Aransas after the hurricane. They **pulled the brand colors directly from that image** — the palette comes from the natural beauty of their island.

They designed a **flag featuring an upright south Texas palm tree** — deliberately straight and not leaning, symbolizing resilience and strength (palm tree roots grow stronger after storms). The name derives from "The Republic of Texas," evoking independence and self-reliance.

## Brand Identity
- **Primary colors**: Navy blue + Coral (sunset-derived)
- **Accent colors**: Gold/yellow, green
- **Central motif**: Upright south Texas palm tree silhouette — "The Original Flag of Port Aransas"
- **Aesthetic**: Elevated coastal lifestyle — preppy, heritage-inflected, not surf/skate
- **Mood**: Warm, proud, resilient, community-rooted

## Product Lines
- **Flags** — The Original Flag of Port Aransas (their most iconic product)
- **Caps** — Sportsman Mesh Flag Caps, Rope Caps, Classic Flag Baseball Caps
- **Apparel** — Casual beachwear to chic accessories
- **Palm Republic Classics** — Heritage collection
- **Home Goods** — Embroidered throw pillows, Turkish bath towels, M-Clip money clips
